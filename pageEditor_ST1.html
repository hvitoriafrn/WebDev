The following pages were created by me:

  • Splash Screen: The splash screen is the first screen that users see when they visit the application. It features the website title, mission and the names of my group members. After a brief "loading" time, it automatically redirects users to the Home page. It contains an animated image that indicates that the home page is loading.
  • Progress: Based on the UN website, I created a content page with the purpose to show the progress towards the targets for "Life on Land".
  • Shop: The shop page allows users to browse and purchase items to support the Life on Land cause.
    • The products are related to the theme.
    • The user can choose the product quantity, size, and color.
    • Each item listing includes an "Add to cart" button.
    • A basket to capture the information about the selected products, including info.
    • Proceeding to checkout after the user selects at least one product.
    • Product checkout section, to finalize purchase and provide customer info.
    • The system provides a message to confirm the completion of the order.
